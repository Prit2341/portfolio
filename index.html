Traffic Management System

In My Traffic Management System, powered by AI, My primary goal is to minimize time spent in traffic by intelligently coordinating traffic flow. Through AI algorithms, we seamlessly synchronize traffic signals, preventing unnecessary stops at multiple intersections.

Leveraging image detection technology, we dynamically allocate time to each lane based on real-time traffic density. The issue at hand is the congestion resulting from the escalating urban vehicle ownership and excessive waiting time at traffic signals. Traditional solutions involve manual control, automatic timer-based systems, or expensive sensor deployments, all of which have their drawbacks

My proposed system offers a dynamic alternative. By autonomously computing optimal green signal times based on current traffic conditions, it reduces delays and congestion. Furthermore, it's cost-effective, leveraging existing infrastructure like CCTV cameras for real-time traffic density calculation. This AI-driven, edge computing solution not only minimizes traffic complexities but also provides a seamless, efficient, and cost-effective traffic management solution.
